Participate in the Industry 4.0 Student Contest 2025

February - June 2025. For brave students only.

The project

Digital Industries World, in collaboration with Politecnico di Milano, University of Brescia, and Fondazione Politecnico di Milano, offers a contest where students can develop an innovative Industry 4.0 application.

The challenge

The competition offers attractive prizes and is open to engineering students. It aims to introduce them to the professional challenges of Industry 4.0 by developing a cloud application.

Technical details

Students will present their projects to a panel of experts from universities and industry. All participants will receive a certificate, and the top 10 teams will receive awards.

  • Predictive maintenance
  • Production management
  • Energy monitoring
  • Cognitive/Physical ergonomics

Timeline

Student registrations by February 28th, 2025

Contest introduction and training March 2025

Project development March to May 2025

Winners award ceremony May 2025
