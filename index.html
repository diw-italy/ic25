The project

Digital Industries World, in promotion with Fondazione Politecnico di Milano and Politecnico di Milano (Dipartimento di Ingegneria Gestionale),

in collaboration with University of Brescia (Dipartimento di Ingegneria dell’Informazione), University of Bergamo (Dipartimento di Ingegneria Gestionale, dell'Informazione e della Produzione), and LIUC – Università Carlo Cattaneo (Scuola di Ingegneria Industriale), offers a contest where students can propose an innovative Industry 4.0 application.

The challenge

The competition offers attractive prizes and is open to engineering students. It aims to introduce them to the professional challenges of Industry 4.0 by developing a cloud application.

Technical details

Students will present their projects to a panel of experts from universities and industry. All participants will receive a certificate, and the top 10 teams will receive awards.

  • Predictive maintenance
  • Production management
  • Energy monitoring
  • Cognitive/Physical ergonomics
